Company

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. The company produces consumer electronics,personal computers, and software. Apple is the world's largest company by market capitalization as of March. Apple's product portfolio includes iPhone, iPad, Mac, iPod, Apple Watch,and Apple TV.

Buildier

Apple was founded by Steve Wozniak, Steve Jobs and Ronald Wayne on April 1, 1976, as Apple Computer Company to develop and sell Wozniak's Apple I personal computer. It was incorporated by Jobs and Wozniak as Apple Computer, Inc. in 1977. Jobs and Wozniak co-founded Apple, introducing first the Apple I and then the Apple.

Production Dipartment

Apple’s Product Development Process may be one of the most successful design processes ever implemented. With a valuation that exceeds $2 trillion, there’s a lot that designers can learn from Apple and introduce into their own design environments.

Symbol

The Apple logo incorporates the symbol of an apple, which has long represented knowledge, wisdom, and temptation1. The original logo, designed by company co-founders Ronald Wayne and Steve Jobs, featured an illustration of Sir Isaac Newton sitting under an apple tree, where he was inspired to develop the theory of gravity2.
